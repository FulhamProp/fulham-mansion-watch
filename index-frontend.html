Why It Matters

What is the £2m mansion tax?

From April 2028, homes valued at £2m+ face a new High Value Council Tax Surcharge — starting around £2,500/year and rising with value. Hammersmith & Fulham is named as one of the boroughs most exposed. If your street's average is in the amber or red zone above, it's worth understanding whether your specific home could be assessed — and whether timing a sale matters.
