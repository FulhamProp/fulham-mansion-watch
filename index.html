Why It Matters

The £2m mansion tax — explained simply.

From April 2028, every home valued by the VOA at £2m or above will pay a new High Value Council Tax Surcharge — starting at around £2,500/year and rising with value. Hammersmith & Fulham has more homes in this bracket than almost any other borough outside Kensington. The question for owners near the line isn't just "what's it worth" — it's whether timing a sale makes financial sense.
